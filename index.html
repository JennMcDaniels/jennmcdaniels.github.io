About Us

Hi! I'm Jennifer! I love being behind and seeing the world from behind the lens. I deliver quality and professional photography services, specializing in family, senior pictures, engagements, and weddings. I also photograph special events, so feel free to reach out if you are looking for something specific. When those important milestones happen in life, it's important to capture them! I'd love to capture them so that you'll be able to look back on those special memories. Call me to get scheduled! I cannot wait to capture your moments that matter!

My photography style provides both posed, candid and prompt-based suggestions to provide a fun experience, capture emotion for a creative collection of photos. The style may differ for your session based on your style. My commitment to quality and customer service is unwavering. Your session is unique to you and can fit your personal style. My goal is to capture the unique personalities of everyone I photograph -and create something beautiful!
